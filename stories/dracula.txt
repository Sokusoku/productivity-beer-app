Title: Dracula
Author: Bram Stoker
Summary: The archetypal vampire novel, telling the story of Count Dracula's attempt to move from Transylvania to England so that he may find new blood and spread the undead curse, and of the battle between Dracula and a small group of men and a woman led by Professor Abraham Van Helsing.
